SURVIVE

Survive is a game of survival against dangerous foes looking to harm you.

A dangerous being appears in a box,and you have to quickly click on it to kill it before you are harmed.

Every time you successfully click on the dangerous being that appears, it is a hit, and you get 10 points.

Every time you miss clicking on the dangerous being, it is considered a miss, and you get 0 point.

If you have 3 misses, the game will be over.

Upon successful completion of a level, you move to the next level, where the dangerous beings are faster.

If you somehow survive all levels,I seriously doubt this!!!You would have completed the game!!!

Good luck.You will need it!!!
